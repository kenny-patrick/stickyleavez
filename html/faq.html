Delta 8

What is Delta 8 THC

Like CBD, Delta 8, written as Δ8 is a cannabinoid found in the cannabis plant. It is found in small concentrations and extracted in concentratred form.

What is the difference between Δ8THC and Delta Δ9THC?

Let's talk chemistry. The term "delta" references a compound molecular structure's double bond. The difference between the two lies in the location of that double bond on the carbon chain.

How will it make me feel?

You will definitely feel the effects. It comes with a "high" similar to Δ9THC, but not as intense. It will definitely take you there in large doses. Users have also stated that they do not experience any anxiety or paranoia found when consuming Δ9THC.

Will I still be able to pass a drug test?

Short answer-probably not. Most modern drug tests detect the presence of THC. They can't differentiate between Δ8 and Δ9 THC.

Wait, so is it illegal?

The 2018 Hemp Farm Bill allows for the manufacturing and sale of any product derived from hemp containing less than 0.3% Delta-9-THC. Although it is federally illegal, some states have banned these products on their own. These states include Alaska, Arizona, Arkansas, Colorado, Delaware, Idaho, Iowa, Mississippi, Montana, Rhode Island, and Utah.

Is this similar to CBD?

The main difference between the CBD and Δ8THC cannibinoids is that Δ8THC is psychoactive. This means it is possible for the consumer to feel the effects.

What types of Delta 8 THC products are available?

Because Delta 8 is found in small amounts, it is not availble in the form of flower. You can however, enjoy it in vape cartridges, distillate syringes, oil, edibles, drinks, concentrate, and tinctures.
